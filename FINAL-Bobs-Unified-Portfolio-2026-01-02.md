## VIII. Decision Rationale Summary

### Why ETFs for Longevity/Infrastructure

1. **Unfamiliar with individual stocks** in these sectors
2. **Reduces single-stock concentration risk** (LLY was 16%)
3. **Maintains theme exposure** without stock-picking risk
4. **Simpler to manage** and rebalance
5. **Still own key stocks indirectly** (LLY ~12% of XLV, ISRG ~10% of IHI)

### Why Add Aerospace/Defense (ITA)

1. **Diversification** into fourth distinct theme
2. **Familiar industry** - understand the news, can hold through volatility
3. **Strong secular tailwinds** - defense spending, aerospace recovery
4. **Uncorrelated** to tech holdings
5. **Strong historical returns** (~12-14% CAGR)

### Why Keep Individual Tech Stocks

1. **Highest conviction** - understand these businesses deeply
2. **Clear moats** - each has defensible competitive position
3. **AI exposure** across different vectors (GPUs, cloud, software, security)
4. **Comfortable holding** through volatility
